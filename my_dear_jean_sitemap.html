Time Out Labone write-up — key quotes to reference

From the Time Out 2025 ranking: Labone described as a magnet for "arty professionals and expats," home to happy-hour pubs, galleries, wine bars, high-end shops, the new Limbo design & architecture museum, and an "ever-buzzing nightlife." Was a sleepy residential area in 2008 — now one of Accra's most dynamic postcodes. Source: timeout.com

Homepage Hero — Dear Jean Inspired

Aesthetic and copy direction

Design note

Design direction

  • 1Full-bleed hero image or autoplay video — building exterior or rooftop view of Labone.
  • 2Minimal overlay nav — logo top left, 3–4 nav links top right, thin white text on dark.
  • 3Headline in elegant serif font, centred or left-aligned: e.g. "Home in the World's Coolest Neighbourhood."
  • 4One CTA button: "Explore Apartments" or "Check Availability."
  • 5Scroll arrow to next section.
Reference: dear-jean.co.za — clean luxury, serif type, high-contrast photography, minimal UI chrome. Avoid heavy graphic elements or flashy animations.

  • ✦Mobile-first. Many Accra users browse on mobile.
